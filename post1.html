ॐ असतो मा सद्गमय ।
Om Asato Maa Sad-Gamaya |
Meaning: Om, (O Lord) From the Phenomenal World of Unreality, make me go (i.e. Lead me) towards the Reality of Eternal Self
तमसो मा ज्योतिर्गमय ।
Tamaso Maa Jyotir-Gamaya |
Meaning: From the Darkness of Ignorance, make me go (i.e. Lead me) towards the Light of Spiritual Knowledge
मृत्योर्मा अमृतं गमय ।
Mrtyor-Maa Amrtam Gamaya |
Meaning: From the World of Mortality of Material Attachment, make me go (i.e. Lead me) towards the World of Immortality of Self-Realization
ॐ शान्तिः शान्तिः शान्तिः ॥
Om Shaantih Shaantih Shaantih ||
Meaning: Om, Peace, Peace, Peace.
